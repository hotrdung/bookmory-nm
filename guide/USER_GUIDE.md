# 📖 Bookmory User Guide

Welcome to **Bookmory**, your personal reading companion! Bookmory helps you track your reading progress, take notes with AI magic, and create beautiful reports to share with your family.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Login
When you open Bookmory, you'll be greeted with a friendly login screen.
1. Click **Sign in with Google**.
2. If you are one of the allowed kids or parents in your family, you'll be let right in!

---

## 📚 Exploring Your Library

Bookmory has two main views to help you find your books.

### My Books vs. Family Library
- **My Books**: Shows only the books you are currently reading.
- **Family Library**: Shows every book your family has added. You can pick any book from here to start your own reading journey!

### View Modes
- **Grid View**: See large, beautiful book covers.
- **List View**: A compact list showing the title, author, and your reading progress.

### Finding Books
- **Search**: Type a title or author in the search bar.
- **Filter**: Tap the tags (like "Magic", "Adventure", or "Science") to see books in those categories.
- **Sort**: Sort your list by "Last Updated", "Date Added", or "Progress" to see what needs your attention.

---

## ✍️ Managing Your Books

### Adding a Book
Tap the **"+" button** at the bottom of the screen.
- **AI Assist**: Type a book title, and let Gemini find the cover, author, and even create a table of contents for you!
- **Manual**: Fill in the details yourself if it's a very special book.

### Editing & Deleting
Parents can edit book details or remove books from the library by clicking the **Edit** or **Trash** icons in the book detail view.

---

## 📈 Your Reading Journey

### Starting a Book
Find a book in the **Family Library** and tap **"Start Reading"**. You'll be asked how many pages the book has and what your daily reading goal is.

### Check-In
Found time to read? Tap the **"Check-In"** button!
- You can log how many pages you read today.
- Or simply tell the app what page you are on now.
- Bookmory will calculate your progress and tell you if you hit your goal!

### Chapters & Checklist
If a book has a Table of Contents, you can check off chapters as you finish them. Some books even auto-check chapters based on the page number you've reached!

### Smart Notes & AI Capture
Want to remember a special part?
1. Go to the **Chapters** tab and tap the **Note** icon.
2. **Type** your thoughts, or...
3. Use **Smart Capture**! Tap the **Camera icon** to take a photo of a page. Gemini will read the text for you and save it as a note!

---

## 🏆 Reading Reports

When you've made progress or finished a book, you can create a **Reading Report**.
Tap the **Printer icon** in the book's "All Notes" section.

### Two Ways to Report:
1. **AI Summary**: Gemini will read all your notes and write a cute, one-page report about your journey with the book. It’s perfect for showing off what you’ve learned!
2. **Full Notes**: A clean, chronological list of every note you've taken, organized by page number.

**Pro Tip**: You can **Print** these reports directly to keep them in a real-life reading folder!

---

## 🎨 Personalization

### Themes & Profiles
Tap the **Menu icon (three lines)** in the header to switch between family members. Each member has their own:
- **Icon**: Choose from a flower, heart, rocket, or galaxy!
- **Color Theme**: From soft pinks to deep space blues.
- **Reading List**: Your "My Books" list is unique to you!

### Stop Reading
If you decide a book isn't for you right now, you can tap **"Stop Reading & Remove"** at the bottom of the book detail. This removes it from your active list but keeps it in the library for later.
